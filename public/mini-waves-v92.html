How the water works

⛵ Vessel — one life area at a time, or All Vessels to see the whole sea. Switch in the header, add your own, switch OFF the ones you don't sail.

Categories — the row under the header is your Vessel's places, by name. ALL shows everything.

〜 Motion — the heart of it: group the day by HOW work happens — calls, errands, ops, dev.

◔ Time · 👁 View — All is your live water; Completed is the logbook. Today / Week / Overdue / a date range you name · Task, Outlook, or Calendar — day · week · month, the schedule that is exactly that. Outlook shows every Vessel: bright is active, click a dim one to wake it.

Bottom bar — filters, Location → ABCDE. They stack. Empty ones hide.

🔍 — search everything. Pick a place and the whole path sets itself.

☰ — your stuff window: Lists, the LEDGER worksheet, Contacts, Notes. +NEW adds a Task, List, Section, or Contact.
